📚 What do these mean?
Crystallinity
How organized the polymer chains are. High crystallinity = chains packed tightly together = stiffer, stronger material.
Plasticizer (Glycerin)
Small molecules that push chains apart. More plasticizer = softer, stretchier material.
Chain Length
How long polymer molecules are. Longer chains = stronger, tougher material.
